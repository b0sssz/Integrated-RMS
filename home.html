Research Management System (RMS)

Organize Your Data Better

A Research Management System (RMS) is a platform for principal investigators to assist them in managing and keeping track of all research activities such as journals, conferences, intellectual property, innovation exhibitions, funds, and expenses. The centralized web application allows easy accessibility of grant information for all the stakeholders. The business process automation engine helps in expediting the work flow process and making it paperless & contactless.
